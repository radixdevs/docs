Three ways to integrate, same infrastructure underneath. Start with what fits your team today. Can consult your Bizdev partner to evaluate what would suit your needs the best.

## <Icon icon="arrow-pointer" color="#83F891" size={30} /> Low Lift

Get up and running in quickly with our hosted flow that helps you test waters.

### You build:

A button or link in your product.

### Namify handles:

Search UI, checkout, billing, domain management, and support.

### Best for:

Testing demand, small teams, MVPs.

<Icon icon="alarm-clock" />  <kbd>\<1 week</kbd>

---

## <Icon icon="window" color="#83B9F7" size={30} /> Hybrid

The best of both worlds. You build a native registration & billing experience on your platform with domain management and support handled by us.

### **You build:**

Search, registration, and billing UX (calling our APIs).

### **Namify handles:**

Renewals, expiry comms, ICANN/registry compliance.

### **Best for:**

Platforms wanting branded UX without operational overhead.

<Icon icon="alarm-clock" /> <kbd>1-2 weeks</kbd>

<Note>
  Optional:\
  Hosted billing with automated dunning, payment retry, and failed payment recovery.
</Note>

---

## <Icon icon="layer-group" color="#F79E83" size={30} /> Full API

Total control. You own the entire user experience — search, checkout, billing, and domain management — all built natively into your product. Namify operates silently in the background, handling the registrar infrastructure so you don't have to.

### **You build:**

Everything user-facing. Domains feel native to your product.

### **Namify handles:**

Registry operations, ICANN accreditation, registrar compliance.

### **Best for:**

Larger partners who can provide their own support.

<Icon icon="alarm-clock" /> **<kbd>30\+ days</kbd>**

<Note>
  Optional:\
  White-label management panels. Custom API modifications possible.
</Note>
